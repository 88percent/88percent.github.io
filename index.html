Empathy and Corruption

By Alexander Bogobowicz, Joey Cormier, Jackson Hollie, Neal Schobbe, and Rhys Wilding


Theme

People that come into positions of power will inevitably be corrupted by their power.


Thesis

Authors such as Arthur Miller, Edgar Allan Poe, and Henry Thoureau have examined their society and found human nature to be such that empathy is lost when power is gained
